My Current Location: Canada


A picture of the Canadian flag.

Canada is a country located in North America. Canada is along the border of the United States. From the north to the south it reaches over half the Norther Hemisphere. From east to west it measures over 7560 kilometers that span to six different time zones. It is known as the second largest country in the world while having a very small population of 0.5% of the worlds population. Canada is known for its famous landmarks, history, food, culture, and outdoor activities.

Learn about the history of Canada!

Fun facts about Canada:
  • Canada has access to 20% of the world's fresh water.
  • Canada has the longest highway in the world.
  • Canada is recognized as one of the friendliest countries in the world.
  • Toronto is the biggest city in Canada.
  • Canada holds the oldest rocks on earth. (Around 4.28 billion years old!)
Top 3 cities to visit:
  1. Toronto, Ontario
  2. Vancouver, British Columbia
  3. Calgary, Alberta

Canadian slang that everyone uses:
Timmies:
Timmies is short for a fast-food/coffee resturaunt called "Tim Hortons".
Be sure to try out some timbits if it's your first time visiting Canada!
Double Double:
A double double reffers to a coffee order from Timmies.
A double double means that you would like two creams and two sugars in your coffee.
The 6ix:
The 6ix is reffered to as the city of Toronto.
A saying popularized by the canadian rapper Drake.
