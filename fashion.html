The above photo is what the current 2022 fashion trend. (Known as streetwear)

Fashion trends are often reflected by celebritis such as artists, actors, and etc. Fashion is a way of communication it doesen't matter what your interest in life are. Descriptions of the top 3 streetwear types:
Streetwear:
Casual clothing fashion for people that are in to feeling comfortable
while staying fashionable. Streetwear is constantly evolving to many
differemt cultures and parts of the world.
Techwear:
Techwear is meant to be modern. It focuses on style and functionality.
Clothing such as vests or cargo pants are often paired with this type
of fashion.
Hypebeast:
A hypebeast is described as someone who usually finds expensive clothes
to show to other people that their clothes hold alot of value.
A hypebeast will rarely wear clothes that are inexpensive.
