My Favorite Video Game: VALORANT


A thumbnail of the video game VALORANT.

VALORANT was developed by Riot Games. The Creator of a popular game called League of Legends decided to make a FPS game. Through the years Riot Games came up with VALORANT. It is an extremely entertaining game to play with several different agents to pick from and includes cool looking cosmetics. VALORANT is a 5 vs 5 player game where players will take turns defending a site or attacking a site. Each game consists of 24 rounds or if you exceed overtime it can take over 24 rounds. The first team to win 13 rounds wins the game. Each team must play 12 rounds of attack or defence. Each player must select a unique agent to play and each of these agents have responsibilities to help get on to the site they are attacking or to defend a site they are defending.

Player roles in VALORANT:

Duelist:
The duelist is meant to create space for the team on the attacker side.
They are able to get onto the site quickly for their team to execute strategies.
Initiators:
Initiators are meant to reveal the location of where people are playing.
They can reaveal where enemies may be hiding to help the team when going into battle.
Controllers:
Controllers have the ability to throw smokes on any location of the map.
They can help their team by denying the vision of their enemies
and by taking control of an area.
Sentinels:
Sentinels help support the team by providing information and prevent flanking enemies.
